Kennel History

Behind the name Sky Licker lies more than 10 years of professional dog breeding experience. My work with serious breeds such as American Staffordshire Terriers and Pitbulls has established strict standards of responsibility for every line in the pedigree.

Captivated by the calmness, beauty and incredible stature of Chow Chows, I made this breed the center of my universe. Our goal is to produce top‑quality breeding dogs of different colors and coat types that combine impeccable exterior and stable temperament.

Health & Genetics

We do not compromise. All our dogs undergo mandatory genetic tests and have official X‑rays confirming the absence of dysplasia.

Family Life

Our Chow Chows live in a country house as full members of the family (one of our greatest prides is an amazing dog named Margo). Puppies grow up in a pack from the first days, receiving ideal socialization.

My name is Irina, and I personally oversee every stage: from breeding planning to careful delivery of puppies anywhere in the world.
